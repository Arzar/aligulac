Staff

These are some of the people who make Aligulac possible. There is some delay in putting new contributors up here, so it might not be 100% complete.

Eivind "TheBB" Fonn

TheBB is the head honcho of Aligulac. He conceived of the site, built the first version, and implemented most of the features currently available. Nowadays, time is shorter, but he remains responsible for the rating system and the inferences (predictions). He is also the server owner and the one paying for the upkeep.

TheBB is Norwegian, but has spent his last four years in the flowery fields of Switzerland.

C. "Conti" Kolbe

Together with Grovbolle, Conti was the first to join the team. He has made innumerable contributions to the database, with the second most matches added, and together with Grovbolle and kiekaboe basically created the event catalogue from scratch. However, Conti is also the man to thank for several features on the site, primarily earnings, team transfers, and several other minor enhancements.

Grovbolle is one of the earliest contributors. After working a lot with the database maintenance in the beginning, he has now moved to more of a public relations role. Today he spends most of his time tweeting obscenities and the occasional useful factoid from @Sc2Aligulac. He also recruits new volunteers and tries to keep them from blowing the site up.

Grovbolle is Danish, and has just completed his Bachelor thesis in Business Administration and Information Technology.

Tom "kiekaboe" van Vliet

Nobody has added more matches than kiekaboe. Man or machine? Does it really matter? Whatever he is, we can thank him for thousands of matches added, probably tens of thousands catalogued, and innumerable conflicts and inconsistencies resolved. The database would not have been the same without him.

One of the first to join the operation, kiekaboe remains active to this day.

William "Inflicted"

Inflicted is a long time database maintainer, and an even longer time Liquipedia contributor who joined the team in January 2013. As our only Aussie member, he is often the one adding SEA results.

His encyclopedic knowledge of Liquipedia and TLPD come in handy when conflicts or inconsistencies are discovered.
